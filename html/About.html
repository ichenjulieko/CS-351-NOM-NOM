A Little About Us

So what are we all about? Well here at the NOM Palace we love food, and we like to think that food loves us too. Because otherwise this would be a very sad and one-sided relationship and we'd feel guilt tripped into quitting food forever and dying which would be bad. Our goal is to just spread the love of food to all corners of the globe and hopefully not make anyone fat in the process, but that's what our healthy section is for. So sit back, grab a pillow or teddy bear or baby squirrel, and get a cup ready to catch your rivers of drool as you feast your eyes on what we have for you here!
